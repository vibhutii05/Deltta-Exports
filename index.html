Where Innovation Drives Global Commerce

Deltta Exports is a dynamic export, supply, and trading company headquartered in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. We specialize in the international trade of high-quality FIBC bags, incense sticks (agarbatti), and Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) and many more products. Established with a vision to provide seamless global trade solutions, we aim to support businesses across diverse sectors by delivering reliable, cost-effective, and timely products.
